Security Access Policy

Employees must use multi-factor authentication for email, payroll, and all internal systems. If a user loses access to their authenticator app, they should contact the service desk and verify their identity with a manager-approved recovery code.

Password resets are available through the self-service portal. Passwords must be at least twelve characters and cannot reuse the previous five passwords.

Privileged access is reviewed every quarter. Managers are responsible for approving or removing access during the review window.
